HTML5 Sketch Pad

An easy-to-use sketch pad with HTML5 and canvas.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

The Point

For quick line doodling and picture sketching in a canvas environment.

No Clicks

Click in the box, drag out and let go. To continue drawing continuously just move the mouse back inside the box.
